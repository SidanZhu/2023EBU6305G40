Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding is a world-famous ex-situ conservation base, research and breeding base, public education base and educational tourism base for giant pandas. The base covers an area of 1000 mu. As the "Giant Panda ex Situ Ecological Protection Demonstration Project", it is famous for protecting and breeding endangered wild animals unique to China such as giant panda and red panda.
