  • Name: Giant Panda Breeding Center
  • Grade:
  • Address: 1375 Panda Avenue, Chenghua District, Chengdu City
  • Tel: 028-83510033
  • Opening Hour: 7:30—18:00

Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding is a world-famous ex-situ conservation base, research and breeding base, public education base and educational tourism base for giant pandas. The base covers an area of 1000 mu. As the "Giant Panda ex Situ Ecological Protection Demonstration Project", it is famous for protecting and breeding endangered wild animals unique to China such as giant panda and red panda.

The Details

  • Name: Du Fu's Cottage
  • Grade:
  • Address: 37 Qinghua Road, Qingyang District, Chengdu City
  • Tel: 028-87345202
  • Opening Hour: 9:00—18:00

Du Fu Cottage is the former residence of Du Fu, a great poet of the Tang Dynasty, when he lived in Chengdu. Du Fu lived here for nearly four years and wrote more than 240 poems. Wei Zhuang, a poet in the late Tang Dynasty, found the ruins of the cottage and rebuilt it to preserve it. It was repaired and expanded in Song, Yuan, Ming and Qing dynasties.
